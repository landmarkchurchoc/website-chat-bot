# Landmark Church: gathering times and core facts (authoritative)

This file is the authoritative source for Landmark's service/gathering times
and basic facts. Some older pages on the website (especially archived sermon
posts and YouTube livestream descriptions) still list outdated times such as
"Wednesday at 6:00pm" for the main gathering. Those are stale. When website
content disagrees with the times below, the times below are correct.

## Main gatherings

- Sunday: 9:00am and 11:00am
- Wednesday: 6:30pm (not 6:00pm)

## Family Life Ministries (these are separate from the main gatherings)

- Jr. High Youth: Wednesdays at 6:00pm, Family Life Center
- Sr. High Youth: Sundays at 6:00pm, Family Life Center
- Co-Op: Thursday mornings during the semester
- Landmark Kids meets during the Sunday and Wednesday gatherings

Note: the 6:00pm times above belong to youth ministries, not the main
Wednesday gathering. Do not describe the main Wednesday gathering as 6:00pm.

## Location

- Main campus: 300 E 8th St., Ocean City, NJ 08226
- Family Life Center: West Ave, Ocean City, NJ (youth gatherings)
