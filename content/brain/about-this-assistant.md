# About this AI assistant

Landmark Church's website offers an AI-powered answer summary above search
results. (Note: the church is always called "Landmark Church" or "Landmark",
never "The Landmark Church".) It answers from the church's website, the church's internal knowledge
base, the ESV Bible, and a short list of trusted ministries approved by
Landmark's leadership (GotQuestions.org and John Piper's content at
DesiringGod.org for quotable material; Enduring Word, Answers in Genesis, and
Logos as link-only recommendations).

It is a starting point, not a substitute for Scripture, the local church, or a
pastor. Anyone with a personal or urgent need should contact the church
directly through the care request form.
